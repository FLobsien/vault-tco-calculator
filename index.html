    Cluster size
    Clients (apps, services, users)

    Anything that authenticates to Vault. Typical: ~25 (startup) · ~150 (mid-market) · 800+ (enterprise).

    Per-client fee / mo

    HCP list price is $72.92 per client / month. Toggle on to enter a negotiated or Enterprise rate (often higher).

    Client-period multiplier

    HCP can count a client for every active period, not once. 1× = unique clients; raise it if identities churn across periods.

    Clusters / environments

    Prod, non-prod, and per-region replicas each multiply cost. Typical: 1 (startup) · 2–3 (mid) · 4+ (enterprise / multi-region).

    Add-on modules & premium support

    % on top of license. Namespaces, HSM/FIPS, DR & replication, Sentinel, and KMIP require Vault Enterprise; paid support tiers add more.

    Operational overhead
    Engineers dedicated to Vault (FTE)

    Running and maintaining Vault. Typical: 0.5 (lean) · 1–2 (mid-market) · 3–5+ (enterprise platform team).

    Fully-loaded engineer cost / yr

    Base + benefits + overhead. US platform/security engineers commonly land $180k–$260k loaded.

    Effort spent on upgrades & maintenance

    Share of engineering time on upgrades, patching, and DR — the most-cited Vault burden. Typical: 30–50% for self-managed.

    Time to production (0→1)

    One-time ramp, charged in year 1. Typical: weeks for a single cluster · 3–6 months for multi-region / HA.

    Professional services / onboarding fee

    One-time, charged in year 1. Vendors commonly attach these to enterprise deployments.

    Growth & renewal
    Annual growth (clients & team)

    Compounds clients and team across the 3-year view. Typical: 15–30% / yr for a scaling team.

    Renewal / hidden-cost uplift

    Procurement data shows 25–60% at renewal — and real cases run higher. Default is the floor.
